Plot

Nine noble families fight for control over the lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millennia.

A popular misconception developed among viewers of the show that the show-runners David Benioff and D.B. Weiss had rushed the making of the final season so they could move on to make a Star Wars project. While several Star Wars movies were planned at the time, Benioff and Weiss had already mapped out the show’s end in 2013, long before Disney approached them.

Production of the final six episodes took twice as long as a regular season, despite having fewer episodes. Later, the creators signed a deal with Netflix, withdrawing from the Star Wars project altogether—proving it wasn't a top priority.
